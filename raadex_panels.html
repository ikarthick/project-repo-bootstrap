PUR & PIR Panels

Product Overview

PUR and PIR panels is frequently used for external cladding or facades and Buildings, Insulation and signage.

High speed of construction, low-cost, and trouble-free operation are the major advantages

These are mainly used for external and internal Partitions/ Architectural claddings, false ceiling, Signage, Machine coverings, container construction cubicle etc…

Raadex Shelters Pvt ltd Follows Quality management as per Indian standard organization ISO 9001-2015


Product Description


Both PUR & PIR have same chemical composition, raw material stabilizer and activator, but they differ on Fire resistant properties. PIR chemicals disintegrate at 3000°C & PUR at 2000°C and hence PIR Board retain the Properties for a long period during fire hazard.

PIR & PUR Insulated Panels have a low thermal conductivity Co-efficient value of K 0.022 W/m k.(Watts per meter - Kelvin Degrees )

Lower the K value, is lower the heat flow through the product.
