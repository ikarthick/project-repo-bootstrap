RADHEX SHELTERS

We are a flagship Manufacturer of a wide spectrum of quality assured PUF panels, roof panels, PUF porta cabins etc. These products are highly appreciated for their high performance, robust construction and long lasting nature.



RADHEX SHELTERS


RADHEX SHELTERS Pvt Ltd, produces Insulated Panels using two metal sheets with options of PPGL, PPGI, SS304, SS316, Aluminium, GRP (FRP), as exterior sheets with PUF (Polyurethane Foam Rigid), PIR (Polyisocyanurate Foam Rigid), Mineral wool (Rockwool) as core insulation. These Panels are produced under Indian Standards IS 12436, European Standards EN 14509 and German Standards DIN 4102 for Fire resistance.


RADHEX SHELTERS Pvt Ltd serves the Indian Market & Exports. The Insulated panels are supplied to the key segments such as Steel, Power, Automobiles, IT, Pharma, Oil & Gas, Infrastructure Sectors, etc. Also the traditional Cold storage sectors & Modern Cold chain demands including Fruits, Vegetables, Dairy & Allied Products, Meat & Poultry, Sea Food, Food Processing, Beverages, Cold logistics, etc.,
